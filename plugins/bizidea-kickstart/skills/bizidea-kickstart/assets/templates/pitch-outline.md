Every slide uses a **Pyramid-Principle headline**: the conclusion, not the topic.

---

## Slide 1 — Title
- **Headline:** <Company> — <5-word tagline>
- **Supporting:** <founder name>, <city / date>
- **Casting note:** Brand hero shot (Nanobanana slot: hero-pitch)

## Slide 2 — Problem (the Villain)
- **Headline:** "<concrete pain statement with a number>" — e.g., "Bakeries waste 18% of daily production"
- **Supporting:**
  - <evidence point from `01-problem-hunt/bleeding-neck-report.md`>
  - <who feels it and how often>
- **Casting note:** Villain reveal — visualise the pain (Nanobanana slot: problem-scene)

## Slide 3 — Solution (the Weapon)
- **Headline:** "<UVP outcome phrased as a customer result>" — e.g., "We cut that waste by 40% in 6 weeks"
- **Supporting:**
  - UVP in one line from `03-uvp/uvp.md`
  - How the Magic Moment works — 1 sentence
- **Casting note:** Show the Weapon in action (Nanobanana slot: solution-in-use)

## Slide 4 — Why Now
- **Headline:** "<the shift that makes this possible today, not 5 years ago>"
- **Supporting:**
  - Technology shift (e.g., LLM cost collapse)
  - Behaviour shift (e.g., SMEs now use smartphones for business)
  - Regulatory / market shift (if applicable)

## Slide 5 — Market
- **Headline:** "<$X market with Y reachable in 3 years>"
- **Supporting:**
  - TAM: <with source>
  - SAM: <with reasoning>
  - SOM: <3-year obtainable>

## Slide 6 — Competition
- **Headline:** "<axis on which we win>" — e.g., "Competitors optimise; we predict"
- **Supporting:**
  - 2×2 or feature-comparison table
  - Honest — list named competitors, don't hide them

## Slide 7 — Product
- **Headline:** "<a concrete proof of progress>" — e.g., "Live MVP in production, X users"
- **Supporting:**
  - Screenshots from `07-mvp-visuals/screens/`
  - Demo link or cue for live demo segment

## Slide 8 — Business Model
- **Headline:** "<the unit economic that makes this work>" — e.g., "LTV : CAC of 4.2 at scale"
- **Supporting:**
  - Pricing: <from `05-bmc/bmc.md`>
  - Key unit-economic numbers from `06-unit-economics/unit-economics.md`

## Slide 9 — Team
- **Headline:** "<the reason this team wins this market>"
- **Supporting:**
  - 1 line per founder: relevant credential, unfair insight
  - Any advisors, if strong

## Slide 10 — Ask
- **Headline:** "<$ amount for milestone by date>" — e.g., "$500K to reach 1,000 paying bakeries by Q2 2027"
- **Supporting:**
  - Use of funds (3 buckets with %)
  - 12-month milestone the funding delivers
  - Contact: <email>
