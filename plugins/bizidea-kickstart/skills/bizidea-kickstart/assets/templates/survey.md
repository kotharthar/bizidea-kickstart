## Questions

Seven questions. All exploratory or behavioural. No "would you use this?" No Likert on intent.

1. **[behavioural]** <question asking about a recent concrete action>
2. **[behavioural]** <question about current workaround>
3. **[open]** Describe the last time <problem occurred>. What did you do?
4. **[behavioural]** Have you ever paid for anything to help with this? If so, what?
5. **[exploratory]** What's the hardest part about <the problem>?
6. **[exploratory]** If <this pain> was gone tomorrow, what would change in your day?
7. **[contact]** (optional) Email if you'd like to hear when we launch.

## What each question tests

| # | Tests | What a strong signal looks like |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Does the pain actually happen? | Concrete recent incident described |
| 2 | How costly is the workaround? | Workaround involves time, money, or stress |
| 3 | Is the pain memorable? | Respondent recalls specifics without prompting |
| 4 | Willingness to pay (proven) | Yes, paid $X for Y |
| 5 | What to solve first | Same answer from multiple respondents |
| 6 | Size of transformation | Big life change implied |
| 7 | Demand signal | Email left voluntarily |
