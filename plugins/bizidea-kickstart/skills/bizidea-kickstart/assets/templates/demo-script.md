## Live demo choreography (slide 7)

60 seconds. One primary Magic Moment. No feature tours.

1. **0:00 — Open** the URL. State the persona's situation in one sentence.
2. **0:10 — Enter** the primary input.
3. **0:20 — Trigger** the Magic Moment.
4. **0:30 — Pause.** Let the audience see the result. Do not narrate over the reveal.
5. **0:45 — Call out** the outcome. "That's 40% less waste, calculated in 3 seconds."
6. **0:60 — Back** to the deck.
